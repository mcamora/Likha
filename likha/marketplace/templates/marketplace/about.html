Our Vision

To be the leading online marketplace that empowers Filipino artisans and small businesses by providing a dedicated digital platform to showcase their handmade products, connect with a global audience, and preserve cultural heritage

The Likha Story

Likha, meaning "creation" in Filipino, was born from a deep appreciation for the rich artistic traditions of the Philippines.

Our journey began in 2018 when our founder, Isabella Santos, returned to her ancestral home in the Philippines after studying design abroad. She was struck by the incredible craftsmanship she encountered in remote villages—intricate weaving, pottery, woodcarving, and jewelry-making that had been passed down through generations.

Despite their exceptional skill, many artisans struggled to make a living wage, with limited access to markets beyond their local communities. Isabella saw an opportunity to bridge this gap by creating a platform that would connect these talented craftspeople with appreciative customers around the world.

Starting with just five artisan partners from her grandmother's village in Bicol, Likha has grown to support over 200 artisans from across the Philippine archipelago. Each piece in our marketplace tells a story—of cultural heritage, of skilled hands, and of communities working to preserve their traditions in a rapidly changing world.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Likha.

Cultural Preservation

We are committed to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of the Philippines through traditional crafts and techniques.

Fair Compensation

We ensure artisans receive fair payment for their work, enabling them to sustain their livelihoods and communities.

Sustainability

We promote environmentally responsible practices in production and packaging, minimizing our ecological footprint.

Community Empowerment

We invest in artisan communities through education, skills training, and infrastructure support.

Authenticity

We celebrate and verify the authentic origins and techniques behind each handcrafted piece.

Transparency

We maintain open communication about our sourcing, pricing, and impact with both artisans and customers.

Sustainable Materials

We prioritize natural, renewable, and locally-sourced materials in all our products, reducing environmental impact while supporting local supply chains.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

All our packaging is plastic-free, using recycled and biodegradable materials that minimize environmental impact without compromising product protection.

Carbon-Neutral Shipping

We offset the carbon footprint of all our shipments through investments in verified environmental projects in the Philippines.
